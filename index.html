ABOUT

I spent the first 5+ years of my career doing Data Analytics for Johnson & Johnson and The Muse, and overtime came to realize that the part of my job that I enjoyed most was creating complex SQL queries, which required intensive logic problem solving. This led me to seek a career path that would bring me closer to this kind of work, and so I started teaching myself to program for a good 2 months. Through learning Javascript and Ruby I was able to build mini-apps and games, using programming as a channel of expression and a means of challenging how I think. Ultimately I decided to enroll in Fullstack Academy to learn from knowledgeable teachers, participate in individual and group projects, and grow as an engineer alongside my peers.

Since November of 2020, I have been a Software Engineer with a frontend focus at The Mighty. In addition to my passion for creating web apps and games, I am an avid rock climber (gym climber) as well a voracious reader of non-fiction!
